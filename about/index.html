About PrivacyPromo

Some information about the PrivacyPromo website, it's purpose, and who is behind it.

Everything you do on the internet is being logged. We're here to help you keep your privacy & help you stay anonymous.

PrivacyPromo, is dedicated to providing privacy-respecting software & service recommendations to protect against global mass surveillance & unethical tracking. Privacy.Promo is a fork of PrivacyTools, but with updated & new recommendations and various other improvements. This service is forever free, displays no ads, and is always welcoming new contributors.

We're owned by an organization known as Swivro. Swivro is heavily focused on both privacy and cybersecurity, so theres no need to worry about PrivacyPromo being owned by a corrupt organization.
